Player Behaviour Tracking Paper Collection

This page is a collection of papers in the area of gambling studies known as player behaviour tracking, which use real online gambling transactions to understand player behaviours. Addition to this collection requires the following conditions be met;





*open data repositories are preferred, but this is not always possible so by-request data are included (subject to change).
**this is not to say these studies are not valuable, but they are not strictly player behaviour tracking as defined above.
